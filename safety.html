Our Commitment to Health & Safety

At Mirfa 2 RO Water Operations & Maintenance Company L.L.C., safety is not just a priority—it's a core value. We are committed to protecting our employees, contractors, and the community by implementing best-in-class health, safety, and environmental practices aligned with ENGIE’s global standards.

Our "No Life at Risk" culture promotes proactive behavior, vigilance, and compliance with the 9 Life Saving Rules. We empower our team to speak up, stop unsafe work, and apply risk assessments in all daily operations.

From last-minute risk assessments to sharing vigilance and reporting incidents, our team lives these principles to build a safe, zero-harm environment.
