Company Profile

Mirfa 2 RO Water Operations & Maintenance Company L.L.C. is responsible for the long-term operation and maintenance of the Mirfa 2 Reverse Osmosis Desalination Plant — a strategically important water infrastructure asset in Abu Dhabi’s Al Dhafra region.

Established under a 30-year O&M agreement, the company ensures reliable, safe, and sustainable delivery of 120 million imperial gallons of potable water per day, serving over 210,000 households across the UAE.

About the Project

The Mirfa 2 RO project is an Independent Water Project (IWP) initiated by Emirates Water & Electricity Company (EWEC), in partnership with ENGIE and TAQA. It reflects the UAE’s strategic commitment to decarbonization and water security, leveraging energy-efficient membrane desalination technology.

Why It Matters

As climate resilience and water demand increase, M2 RO represents a cornerstone in the UAE’s water strategy. It minimizes environmental impact, supports public-private collaboration, and provides operational excellence through state-of-the-art facilities.
