Mirfa 2 RO – Ensuring water security for the UAE

About the Project

The Mirfa 2 Reverse Osmosis (M2 RO) Seawater Desalination Plant is a strategic Independent Water Project (IWP) led by Emirates Water & Electricity Company (EWEC), developed in partnership with ENGIE and TAQA. Located in the Al Dhafra Region of Abu Dhabi, the project will supply 120 million imperial gallons per day (MIGD) of potable water, significantly supporting the UAE's long-term water demand and sustainability goals.

The project reflects the UAE’s commitment to adopting energy-efficient, low-carbon desalination technologies and strengthening public-private partnerships in the utility sector. The Mirfa 2 RO Water Operations & Maintenance Company is entrusted with the long-term reliability and performance of this critical infrastructure.
